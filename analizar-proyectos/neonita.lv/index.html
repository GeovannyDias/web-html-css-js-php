ABOUT US

We are the engineering-industrial company engaged in working out, designing, manufacturing and installation of light systems of various level of complexity.

Neonita produces visual lighting and non-lighting advertising on a truly professional level. We are on the visual advertising market since 1999. The company has its own manufacturing of neon, as well as have enough power for the production of large advertisements. Specialists in design, electrics, electronics and advertising and design constructions work in our company.
